Profile

Research, the sea and scientific listening.

With a degree in Marine Sciences and a Master in Acoustic Pollution Management and Assessment from the University of Cádiz, I have more than 20 years of experience blending academic research, technical consultancy and marine field campaigns.

At the University Institute of Marine Research, I design and execute underwater acoustics projects, working with hydrophones, moorings, vector sensors, acoustic records processing, technical reports, and noise assessment studies in marine environments.

Now

Currently active projects.

Ibiza · Formentera

Posidonia Soundscapes: Conservation & Music, Ibiza

A conservation and music project that records and analyzes soundscapes associated with Posidonia seagrass meadows. Approved as a United Nations Decade of Ocean Science action, it connects scientific monitoring, public listening and marine protection.

More project details

La Caleta

Pools as sound shelters

A research line focused on how intertidal rock pools can act as acoustic refuges for small marine organisms. It examines the relationship between soundscape, human pressure and coastal microhabitats.

More project details

Strait of Gibraltar

SEANIMALMOVE

Monitoring movement and population dynamics of marine and coastal vertebrates in the face of anthropogenic impacts and global change. Underwater acoustics enables detection of presence, behavior and sound context of cetaceans in high-traffic areas.
